Hi there! I'm Riza and welcome to my homepage :)

While studying for my bachelor's degree in *Electrical & Electronics Engineering* at [Istanbul Bilgi University](https://www.bilgi.edu.tr/en/), I developed a keen interest in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and I found this very intriguing and exciting, specifically its influence in everyday life of ours and its potential **to make the world a better place**! After my bachelor’s, I pursued a master’s degree in *Intelligent Systems* at [University Bielefeld](https://www.uni-bielefeld.de/), which I completed in *April 2021*.

Thanks to the swift progress of my bachelor’s and master’s degrees, it is glaring that studying and doing research are endeavours I would like to engage in even more! That being said, I'm thrilled to announce that I will be joining the [Hammer Lab ML](https://www.cit-ec.de/en/tcs) at [University Bielefeld](https://www.uni-bielefeld.de/) for my Ph.D. to research Machine Learning for Process Mining! Together with [Prof.Dr.Wil van der Aalst](http://www.padsweb.rwth-aachen.de/wvdaalst/) & [Prof.Dr.Barbara Hammer](https://www.cit-ec.de/en/tcs/barbara-hammer), I will be working on the [ML4ProM Project](https://dataninja.nrw/?page_id=326). This project, along with 6 other projects, is formed inside the research training group [DataNinja](https://dataninja.nrw/) which is funded by the [Ministry of Culture and Science of the State of North Rhine-Westphalia (MKW NRW)](https://www.mkw.nrw/). Please visit [this](https://dataninja.nrw/?page_id=326) website to get more information.

At the same time, I am one of the co-founders of a startup company called [Recommendy](https://www.recommendy.io/), where we build Recommender Systems utilizing Machine Learning algorithms and state-of-the-art Deep Learning techniques.
